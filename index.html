We build what the mission demands — not what the catalogue offers.

Vikasana Systems is an Indian defence and autonomous technology company building integrated hardware-software platforms across land, air, and maritime domains. Every system we ship is designed, engineered, and assembled in India — from embedded firmware to AI inference pipelines.

Our platforms are conceived for low-signature, no-radio-silence operations. Portable. Compact. Powered by edge AI running completely off-grid, consuming a fraction of the energy of comparable Western systems. We are building the autonomous nervous system of the next-generation Indian warfighter.

Operating Domains

Land. Air.
Water.

Vikasana Systems operates across the three primary physical domains of modern conflict and surveillance — building indigenous autonomous platforms for each, engineered to work in concert as a unified multi-domain network.

DOM-01
Land Systems
Unmanned Ground Vehicles (UGV) built for reconnaissance, perimeter security, and logistics support. Autonomous navigation with terrain-adaptive control and silent electric drive. Designed to operate where putting a soldier at risk is unacceptable.
UGV Autonomous Nav Perimeter Security Logistics
DOM-02
Air Systems
Fixed-wing and multi-rotor UAVs designed for ISR, target acquisition, and electronic warfare support. Low acoustic signature, extended endurance, and fully encrypted command-and-control with no radio silence requirement using LPI/LPD waveforms.
UAV ISR Fixed-Wing VTOL LPI/LPD
DOM-03
Water Systems
Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROV) and Unmanned Surface Vessels (USV) for coastal patrol, underwater inspection, mine detection, and maritime domain awareness. Autonomous NMEA-fused navigation with acoustic positioning.
ROV USV Coastal Patrol Mine Detection
DOM-04
Software & AI
Edge-native AI inference, computer vision, and C2 software running entirely on-device — no cloud, no latency, no dependency. Multi-platform GCS, sensor fusion middleware, and autonomous decision layers designed for contested electromagnetic environments.
Edge AI Computer Vision GCS Sensor Fusion
DOM-05
Supporting Equipment
Portable ground stations, encrypted datalink hardware, battery and power management systems, ruggedised sensor packages, and field-deployable edge compute modules — all designed to complement and extend the Vikasana platform ecosystem.
Ground Stations Datalink HW Power Systems Edge Compute
DOM-06
Multi-Domain Network
All Vikasana platforms are designed to interoperate as a unified mesh. A UGV on the ground, a UAV overhead, and an ROV offshore share situational awareness through a common encrypted, low-probability-of-intercept data fabric — with zero cloud infrastructure.
Mesh Networking Multi-Domain Offgrid Encrypted

Engineering Doctrine

How We
Build.

01
No Radio Silence Penalty
Systems engineered around Low Probability of Intercept / Low Probability of Detection (LPI/LPD) communication architectures. Our platforms operate in contested RF environments without exposing position or intent.
02
Edge AI — Zero Cloud
All inference runs on-device. Object detection, target classification, sensor fusion, and autonomous decision-making execute locally on low-power embedded hardware. No latency. No external dependency. No intercept surface.
03
Completely Offgrid
Portable, self-powered platforms with onboard energy management. Designed for extended forward deployment without logistics tail. Smaller, lighter, and drawing a fraction of the power of comparable international systems.
04
Sovereign Stack
From bare-metal firmware on STM32 and i.MX8MP SoCs to GStreamer pipelines, Linux BSP, and application software — the entire stack is developed, owned, and auditable in India. No black-box foreign IP in the critical path.
05
Field Survivability
Ruggedised for high-vibration, temperature extremes, dust, and humidity. Every mechanical and electronic design decision accounts for Indian terrain conditions — from Himalayan high-altitude to coastal humid environments.
