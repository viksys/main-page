Our DNA

FORGED IN INDIA.

We build what the mission demands — not what the catalogue offers.

Vikasana Systems is an Indian defence and autonomous technology company building integrated hardware-software platforms across land, air, and maritime domains. Every system we ship is designed, engineered, and assembled in India — from embedded firmware to AI inference pipelines.

Our platforms are conceived for low-signature, no-radio-silence operations. Portable. Compact. Powered by edge AI running completely off-grid, consuming a fraction of the energy of comparable Western systems. We are building the autonomous nervous system of the next-generation Indian warfighter.

Engineering Doctrine

HOW WE BUILD.

01

No Radio Silence Penalty

Systems engineered around LPI/LPD communication architectures. Operate in contested RF environments without exposing position or intent.

02

Edge AI — Zero Cloud

All inference runs on-device. Object detection, target classification, sensor fusion execute locally on low-power embedded hardware.

03

Completely Offgrid

Portable, self-powered platforms with onboard energy management. Extended forward deployment without logistics tail.

04

Sovereign Stack

From bare-metal firmware on STM32 and i.MX8MP SoCs to GStreamer pipelines — the entire stack developed and owned in India.

05

Field Survivability

Ruggedised for high-vibration, temperature extremes, dust, and humidity. Designed for Indian terrain from Himalayan altitude to coastal humidity.
